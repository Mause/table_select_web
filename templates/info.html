Info

The concept behind this website is moderately simple.

Attendee's of the Margaret River Senior High School Ball o' 2013 select a table from the homepage, enter their name and the name of their partner, and it is stored in the system.

If an attendee makes a choice which they later wish to change, they simply click the small x next to the entry they wish to change. Due to a risk of abuse, the change is not made immediately. The change must be approved by moderators.
